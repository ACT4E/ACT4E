\chaptersecond{Uncertain DPs}{chapter-placeholder}{chap:udp}{
    In this chapter we describe the category \UDP of ``uncertain DPs''.
    We can use \UDP to model uncertainty in the data.
    We can also purposefully introduce uncertainty as a type of ``relaxation''.
    With this strategy, we can find computable approximations of uncomputable DPs.
}
