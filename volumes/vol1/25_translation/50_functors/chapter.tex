\chaptersecond{Translation}{chapter-luxem}{chap:functors}{
    In this chapter we discuss \emph{functors}, which are generalizations of \emph{morphisms} and \emph{actions}.
    Functors are maps from one category to another that ``preserve the structure'', in the sense that the application of a \SY{functor} commutes with category composition.
}
