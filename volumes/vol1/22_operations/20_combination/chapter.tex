\chaptersecond{Combination}{chapter-edelweiss}{ch:combination}{
    This chapter discusses products and co-products, two canonical ways of combining objects in a category.
    In the category \Set, they are cartesian product and disjoint union.
    In a lattice, they are the meet and join.
}
