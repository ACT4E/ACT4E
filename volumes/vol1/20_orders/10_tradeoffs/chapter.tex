\chaptersecond{Trade-offs}{chapter-chocolates}{chap:tradeoffs}{
    Life is about trade-offs: there is seldom a uniformly best outcome; rather we need
    to reason with incomparable attributes.
    Partially ordered sets (posets) are the mathematical structure used to reason about trade-offs.
    They are also important as one of the simplest examples of categories.
}
