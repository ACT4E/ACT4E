\subsubsection*{Representation}

The file format for monoids is an extension of those for semigroups.
There is an extra field \fieldname{neutral} that gives you the neutral element.
\Cref{fig:monoid2} shows an example.

\begin{marginfigure}%
    \datafile{monoid2}{}
    \caption{The simple monoid $\makeset{0,+1}$ with multiplication}%
    \label{fig:monoid2}%
\end{marginfigure}%
