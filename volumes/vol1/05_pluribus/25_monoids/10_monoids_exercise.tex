\classlisting{FiniteMonoid}{}

\subsubsection*{Representation}

The file format for \SY{monoids} is an extension of those for \SY{semigroups}.
There is an extra field \fieldname{neutral} that gives you the \SY{neutral element}.
\Cref{fig:monoid2} shows an example.

\begin{marginfigure}%
    \datafile{monoid2}{}
    \caption{The simple \SY{monoid} $\makeset{0,+1}$ with multiplication}%
    \label{fig:monoid2}%
\end{marginfigure}%
