\chaptersecond{Dependencies}{chapter-grimsel}{chap:dependencies}{
	Another concept that can be modeled naturally by categories is a concept of dependencies.
	If A depends on B, and B depends on C, then C depends on A.
	These could be logical dependencies, like the dependencies between software versions, or could be physical dependencies, like a motor needing electricity.
}
